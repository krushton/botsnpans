About Bots and Pans

The story: You are a robot, and you have been manufactured for the express purpose of serving mankind. Literally. Your hungry human overlord has "hired" you to make him his dinner. But before you can go wild in the kitchen and create the culinary masterpieces your tin robot soul yearns to cook, you have to prove your worth to the overlord. Cook some of his favourite recipes, and maybe you won't end up in the junkyard!

Bots and Pans is a drag-and-drop game created for a User Interface Design class at the UC Berkeley School of Information.
