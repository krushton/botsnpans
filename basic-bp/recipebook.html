You Are the Chef of the Future!

You are Chefbot 3000, a high-tech cooking bot programmed to serve humankind. Literally. Your hungry human overlord has "hired" you to make him his dinner. But before you can go wild in the kitchen and create the culinary masterpieces your tin robot soul yearns to cook, you have to prove your worth to the overlord. Cook some of his favourite recipes, and maybe you won't end up in the junkyard!
